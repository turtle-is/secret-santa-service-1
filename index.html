Secret Santa

Welcome to our Secret Santa gift exchange!

Rules

  • All participants must bring a wrapped gift valued at $20 or less to the gift exchange party.
  • Each participant will draw the name of another participant to determine who they will be buying a gift for.
  • The identity of the person for whom you are buying the gift should be kept a secret until the exchange party.
